Descriptive & Normative Models of Collective Behaviour

School of Mathematics, University of Leeds
June 20-24 2022.

Organisers: Valentin Lecheval, Richard Mann, Anna Sigalou
​​

Schedule
This workshop will bring together recent developments in collective behaviour from theoretical and empirical research. We hope to explore common ground between data-driven approaches utilising sensor technology and statistical analysis, and the latest mathematical & computational modelling research.

The workshop will feature talks by invited speakers on their latest research and discussion groups on open questions.

A prelimary schedule of the workshop is available - please check back here for updates as speakers are confirmed

The workshop is free to attend. Please email Valentin Lecheval to register your interest
